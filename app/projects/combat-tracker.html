About this Project

Run your planned or ad-hoc encounters with full initiative, turn, round, HP tracking, and quick monster stat lookup!

  • Built on top of the Encounter Builder to provide a seamless experience transitioning from planning to playing
  • Undo/redo system
  • Added visual flair to make the UI pop and feel immediately responsive to the user
  • Developed with an accessibility-first, mobile-first mindset
